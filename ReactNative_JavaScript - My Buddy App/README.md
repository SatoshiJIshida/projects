# My Buddy App 
Satoshi Ishida, King's College London Major Group Project.
Contributions - Frontend, Backend; Mobile Notifications and Design.



## Objectives 

The My Buddy App is aimed at doctors who have to make home visits to the patients who are unable to come to the clinic. Sometimes, these visits put their safety at risk due to the unsafe location that they may need to travel to or due to the mental health problems of the patient. Therefore, this mobile application was made at the request of our client, Dr Joshua Harwood who works in South London and Maudsley NHS Foundation Trust to minimize the potential risks and dangerous situations for clinicians.
